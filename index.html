What Is Carbon Fiber?

Carbon atoms are joined in a long chain via chemical bonds to create carbon fiber. The fibers, which have great stiffness, strength, and lightness, are employed in a variety of processes to produce top-notch aftermarket parts for cars. We prefer carbon fiber because of its high tensile strength, stiffness, tolerance, heat resistance, and chemical resistance. Moreover, it is extremely lightweight compared to conventional metal sheets.

What Is Your Return Policy?

Once you have received your order from the delivery date, you will have up to 30 days from that period to return the product. We will inspect the product to make sure there are no damages or other defects. All returns for refunds will incur a 25% restocking fee. The customer pays for return shipping, and store credit will be issued minus the shipping charge for the return label. All products approved for return are eligible for store credit, exchange, or refund. There is no return, refund, or exchange for any parts that have been used, damaged, modified, painted, or installed. Returns are not eligible for custom products such as: steering wheels, grilles, diffusers, front lips, side skirts, exhaust tips, DRL modules, CarPlay products, headlights, tail lights, spoilers, mirror caps, front hoods, rear trunks, engine covers, mid-pipes, downpipes, floor mats, key fobs, seat back covers, interior trim sets, splitters, brace bars, canards, etc. Custom products will be identified by the absence of a quantity counter on the product page. To be eligible for a return, your item must be in the same condition that you received it, unused, and in its original packaging. You will also need the receipt or proof of purchase. Please send a video and photo of the situation, if applicable, to speed up the process.
